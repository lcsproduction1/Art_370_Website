BIO & CONTACT

Linda Schneider Singer has been a designer and content production manager for over 30 years.

Her background includes design, branding and digital and print production including press checks. She specializes in digital print, collateral and web design.

Linda works with her clients as a team member listening to their needs and providing solutions.

Project involvement can be as an additional member of the client side, leading the project on her own from the initial consultation to the finished project or any aspect of the job as shown in her samples.

Linda is based in Glencoe, IL. She enjoys music, painting and teaching martial arts.
